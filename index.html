把实验模板、安排、记录和常用计算集中到一个 App

湿实验助手（WetLab Pro）是一款面向湿实验场景的本地优先效率工具，适合生物、分子、生化及其他湿实验相关场景。

它帮助你减少重复查表、手算和分散记录，把实验准备、执行和复盘流程整理得更清晰。

Bring templates, schedules, logs, and lab tools into one focused app

WetLab Pro is a local-first productivity app built for wet-lab workflows across biology, molecular, biochemistry, and related lab scenarios.

It helps reduce repetitive table lookup, manual calculation, and fragmented note taking so experiment preparation, execution, and review stay clearer.

核心能力

  • 实验模板库：支持内建模板与自建模板，支持分类管理、快速复制、流程型和溶液型编辑
  • 实验安排与提醒：支持实验日程安排、重复规则、快速提醒与通知
  • 补充记录：支持为每次实验安排添加文字和图片
  • 体积与配方辅助：支持按目标体积自动缩放材料用量
  • 常用实验计算：支持摩尔浓度、溶液稀释、分子量与化学式、RPM/RCF 换算
  • 实用工具：支持多任务计时器、盲点计数器和历史记录
  • 数据能力：支持安排与记录导出/恢复，可选开启 iCloud 同步

Core Capabilities

  • Experiment templates: built-in and custom templates, category management, quick duplication, workflow-style and solution-prep editing
  • Experiment schedules and reminders: planning, recurring rules, quick reminders, and notifications
  • Supplemental logs: attach text and images to each scheduled experiment
  • Volume-based scaling: scale material amounts by target volume
  • Lab calculators: molarity, dilution, molecular weight and formula parsing, RPM/RCF conversion
  • Utility tools: multi-timer, blind counter, and history
  • Data continuity: export and restore schedules and logs, with optional iCloud sync

产品特点

  • 本地优先：默认情况下，实验安排、补充记录和图片保存在你的设备上
  • 专注湿实验：围绕实验准备、执行和复盘场景设计
  • 减少重复劳动：把模板、记录、计算和提醒整合到一个 App 中
  • 按需同步：iCloud 同步为可选功能，由用户手动开启或关闭

Product Traits

  • Local-first: schedules, supplemental logs, and images are stored on your device by default
  • Built for wet labs: designed around experiment preparation, execution, and review workflows
  • Less repetitive work: keeps templates, records, calculations, and reminders in one app
  • Sync when needed: iCloud sync is optional and user-controlled
