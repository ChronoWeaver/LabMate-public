WetLab Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-31

Summary

WetLab Pro is local-first. Schedules, notes, and images are stored on your device by default.

Data we process

  • Schedules and reminders you create
  • Log text and images you add
  • App preferences (for example theme and iCloud switch state)

How data is used

  • To provide core app features such as calculations, scheduling, reminders, backup/restore, and log management
  • To keep data available across devices when iCloud sync is enabled by you

Data sharing

  • We do not sell personal data
  • We do not use third-party ad tracking
  • Data is shared only when you explicitly export/share files or enable iCloud sync

Permissions

  • Camera: capture experiment log images
  • Photo Library (read): choose images for logs
  • Photo Library (add): export images
  • Notifications: reminder alerts
  • iCloud: optional sync when enabled in app settings

Retention and control

  • You can delete schedules, logs, and images in the app
  • Backup/restore actions are user-initiated
  • You can disable iCloud sync in app settings at any time
