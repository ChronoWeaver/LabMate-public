隐私政策

最后更新：2026-05-18

欢迎使用湿实验助手（WetLab Pro）。本隐私政策用于说明我们如何处理与你使用本应用相关的数据。

1. 概述

湿实验助手是一款本地优先应用。默认情况下，你的实验安排、补充记录、图片和相关偏好设置保存在你的设备上。

本应用不要求你注册账号才可使用主要功能。部分功能可依赖 Apple 提供的系统能力或服务，例如通知、相册、相机、iCloud 和系统日历。

2. 我们处理的数据

  • 你创建的实验安排、提醒和模板内容
  • 你添加的补充记录文本与图片
  • 应用偏好设置，例如主题模式、iCloud 同步开关状态和系统日历同步开关状态
  • 你主动触发导出、恢复或分享时所涉及的数据文件

3. 数据用途

  • 提供实验模板、实验安排、提醒和补充记录功能
  • 提供配液、浓度、分子量、RPM/RCF 等常用实验工具功能
  • 支持数据导出、恢复和本地数据管理
  • 在你主动开启 iCloud 同步时，实现跨设备同步
  • 在你主动开启系统日历同步时，将实验安排的标题、时间、备注提示和提醒同步到系统日历
  • 维护应用的基本设置与使用体验

4. 数据存储与同步

默认情况下，相关数据保存在你的设备本地。

当你主动开启 iCloud 同步时，相关数据可能通过 Apple 提供的 iCloud 服务在你的设备间同步。你可以随时在应用设置中关闭 iCloud 同步。

当你主动开启系统日历同步时，本应用会在系统日历中创建和维护一个 WetLab Pro 专用日历事件投影。同步到系统日历的内容仅包含实验安排的关键日程信息，例如标题、时间、备注提示和提醒；完整实验安排、实验记录和图片仍保存在应用内。你可以随时在应用设置中关闭系统日历同步，并选择保留或删除已同步到系统日历的事件。

5. 数据共享

  • 我们不会出售你的个人数据
  • 我们不使用第三方广告跟踪
  • 仅在你主动导出、恢复、分享、开启 iCloud 同步或开启系统日历同步时，数据才可能被传输或写入对应系统服务
  • 法律法规要求或有权机关依法要求提供时，可能依法配合提供

6. 权限说明

  • 相机：用于拍摄实验补充记录图片
  • 相册读取：用于选择图片添加到补充记录
  • 相册写入：用于将导出图片保存到系统相册
  • 通知：用于实验安排和提醒通知
  • iCloud：用于你主动开启后的数据同步
  • 日历：用于你主动开启系统日历同步后，将实验安排写入系统日历；本应用不会默认开启日历同步，也不会把完整实验记录或图片写入系统日历

7. 数据保存与控制

你可以在应用内删除实验安排、补充记录和图片。导出与恢复操作均由你主动触发。你也可以随时关闭 iCloud 同步或系统日历同步。关闭系统日历同步时，你可以选择保留已同步的日历事件，或仅删除由 WetLab Pro 创建并记录的已同步事件。

8. 未成年人说明

如你属于未成年人，建议在监护人指导下阅读并使用本应用。监护人应协助你理解本隐私政策，并在必要时作出选择。

9. 政策更新

我们可能根据产品更新、法律法规变化或合规要求对本隐私政策进行调整。更新后的版本将在公开页面或应用内适当位置发布，并自发布之日起生效。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

Welcome to WetLab Pro. This privacy policy explains how we process data related to your use of the app.

1. Overview

WetLab Pro is a local-first app. By default, your experiment schedules, supplemental logs, images, and related preferences are stored on your device.

The app does not require account registration for core features. Some functions rely on Apple-provided capabilities or services such as notifications, Photos, camera, iCloud, and system calendars.

2. Data We Process

  • Experiment schedules, reminders, and template content you create
  • Supplemental log text and images you add
  • App preferences such as theme mode, iCloud sync state, and system calendar sync state
  • Data files involved when you explicitly export, restore, or share content

3. How Data Is Used

  • To provide experiment templates, scheduling, reminders, and supplemental logs
  • To provide lab tools such as molarity, dilution, molecular weight, and RPM/RCF conversion
  • To support export, restore, and local data management
  • To sync data across devices when you explicitly enable iCloud sync
  • To sync schedule titles, times, note hints, and alerts to your system calendar when you explicitly enable system calendar sync
  • To maintain app settings and usability

4. Storage And Sync

By default, relevant data is stored locally on your device.

If you enable iCloud sync, related data may be synchronized across your devices through Apple iCloud. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in settings.

If you enable system calendar sync, the app creates and maintains a lightweight event projection in a dedicated WetLab Pro calendar. Calendar events include only key schedule information, such as title, time, note hint, and alerts; full experiment schedules, records, and images remain in the app. You can disable system calendar sync at any time and choose whether to keep or delete synced calendar events.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data
  • We do not use third-party ad tracking
  • Data may be transferred or written to system services only when you explicitly export, restore, share data, enable iCloud sync, or enable system calendar sync
  • We may comply with lawful requests from regulators or authorities when required by law

6. Permissions

  • Camera: used to capture supplemental log images
  • Photo Library read: used to choose images for supplemental logs
  • Photo Library add: used to save exported images to your library
  • Notifications: used for schedules and reminder alerts
  • iCloud: used only when you enable sync
  • Calendars: used only when you enable system calendar sync, to write experiment schedules to your system calendar. The app does not enable calendar sync by default and does not write full experiment records or images to Calendar.

7. Retention And Control

You can delete schedules, supplemental logs, and images in the app. Export and restore actions are user-initiated, and you can disable iCloud sync or system calendar sync at any time. When disabling system calendar sync, you can choose to keep synced calendar events or delete only events created and tracked by WetLab Pro.

8. Minors

If you are a minor, please review and use the app under guidance from your guardian. Guardians should help you understand this policy and make choices when needed.

9. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy based on product changes, legal updates, or compliance requirements. Updated versions will be posted on the public pages or within the app and take effect upon publication.
