WetLab Pro 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-30

中文

概述

WetLab Pro（湿实验助手）是本地优先应用。默认情况下，你的实验安排、补充记录、图片和相关偏好设置保存在你的设备上。部分功能可依赖 Apple 提供的系统能力或服务，例如通知、相册、相机、iCloud 和系统日历。

我们处理的数据

  • 你创建的实验安排、提醒和模板内容
  • 你添加的补充记录文本与图片
  • 应用偏好设置（例如主题模式、iCloud 同步开关状态和系统日历同步开关状态）
  • 你主动触发导出、恢复或分享时所涉及的数据文件

数据用途

  • 用于提供核心功能，例如实验安排、提醒、备份/恢复、补充记录和常用实验工具
  • 当你主动开启 iCloud 同步时，用于在你的设备间同步数据
  • 当你主动开启系统日历同步时，用于将实验安排的标题、时间、备注提示和提醒同步到系统日历

数据存储与同步

默认情况下，相关数据保存在你的设备本地。当你主动开启 iCloud 同步时，相关数据可能通过 Apple 提供的 iCloud 服务在你的设备间同步。

当你主动开启系统日历同步时，本应用会在系统日历中创建和维护一个 WetLab Pro 专用日历事件投影。同步到系统日历的内容仅包含实验安排的关键日程信息，例如标题、时间、备注提示和提醒；完整实验安排、实验记录和图片仍保存在应用内。

数据共享

  • 我们不会出售你的个人数据
  • 我们不使用第三方广告跟踪
  • 仅在你主动导出/分享文件、主动开启 iCloud 同步或主动开启系统日历同步时，数据才会被传输或写入对应系统服务

权限说明

  • 相机：用于拍摄补充记录图片
  • 相册读取：用于从相册选择图片添加到补充记录
  • 相册写入：用于将导出图片保存到系统相册
  • 通知：用于实验提醒通知
  • iCloud：可选功能，仅在你开启后用于同步
  • 日历：可选功能，仅在你开启系统日历同步后，用于将实验安排写入系统日历；本应用不会默认开启日历同步，也不会把完整实验记录或图片写入系统日历

保存与控制

  • 你可以在 App 内删除实验安排、补充记录和图片
  • 备份与恢复操作均由你主动触发
  • 你可以随时在设置中关闭 iCloud 同步
  • 你可以随时关闭系统日历同步，并选择保留已同步的日历事件，或仅删除由 WetLab Pro 创建并记录的已同步事件

English

Summary

WetLab Pro is a local-first app. By default, your schedules, supplemental logs, images, and related preferences are stored on your device. Some features rely on Apple-provided capabilities or services such as notifications, Photos, camera, iCloud, and system calendars.

Data We Process

  • Experiment schedules, reminders, and template content you create
  • Supplemental log text and images you add
  • App preferences (for example, theme mode, iCloud sync state, and system calendar sync state)
  • Data files involved when you explicitly export, restore, or share content

How Data Is Used

  • To provide core features such as scheduling, reminders, backup/restore, supplemental logs, and lab utilities
  • To sync your data across devices when you explicitly enable iCloud sync
  • To sync schedule titles, times, note hints, and alerts to your system calendar when you explicitly enable system calendar sync

Storage and Sync

By default, relevant data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, related data may be synchronized across your devices through Apple iCloud.

If you enable system calendar sync, the app creates and maintains a lightweight event projection in a dedicated WetLab Pro calendar. Calendar events include only key schedule information, such as title, time, note hint, and alerts; full experiment schedules, records, and images remain in the app.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data
  • We do not use third-party ad tracking
  • Data is transferred or written to system services only when you explicitly export/share files, enable iCloud sync, or enable system calendar sync

Permissions

  • Camera: capture supplemental log images
  • Photo Library (read): choose images for supplemental logs
  • Photo Library (add): save exported images to your library
  • Notifications: reminder alerts
  • iCloud: optional sync, only when enabled by you
  • Calendars: optional sync, only when enabled by you, used to write experiment schedules to your system calendar. The app does not enable calendar sync by default and does not write full experiment records or images to Calendar.

Retention and Control

  • You can delete schedules, supplemental logs, and images in the app
  • Backup/restore actions are user-initiated
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in settings
  • You can disable system calendar sync at any time and choose to keep synced calendar events or delete only events created and tracked by WetLab Pro
