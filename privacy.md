# WetLab Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-31

## Summary

WetLab Pro is designed as a local-first app. Your schedules, notes, and images are stored on your device by default.

## Data We Process

- Experiment schedules and reminders you create
- Supplemental log text and images you add
- App preferences (for example theme and iCloud sync switch state)

## How Data Is Used

- To provide core app features such as calculations, scheduling, reminders, backup/restore, and log management
- To keep your data available across devices when you enable iCloud sync

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell personal data
- We do not use third-party ad tracking
- Data is only shared when you explicitly export/share files, or when you enable iCloud sync

## Permissions

- Camera: capture experiment log images
- Photo Library (read): choose images for logs
- Photo Library (add): export images to your library
- Notifications: reminder alerts
- iCloud: optional sync, only when user enables it

## Retention and Control

- You can delete schedules, logs, and images in the app
- Backup/restore actions are user-initiated
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in app settings
